Hi, I'm Brarian, a full-stack web developer in Austin, Texas.

I aspire to use technology to innovate products and services that positively impact people's lives and bring them joy. The entire development process: from brainstorming a seed of an idea, to exploring potential features for existing applications, to coding full-fledged applications, excites me. Currently, I code using the MERN (MongoDB, Express, ReactJS, NodeJS) stack and I am interested in full-stack or frontend roles. I'm a pop culture fanatic who spends my time outside of work on the water, in the gym, or traveling.

Little Mixer

Little Mixer is an app which guides users through the process of planning and throwing a Dinner or Cocktail Party. Little Mixer is intended to make, entertaining, a process that is seems overwhelming achievable. Entertaining should be easy and fun. Given a plan anyone can be a great host.

Technology Used: React, Redux, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, Enzyme, Mocha, Axios
